# The Villa Tent Homepage Specification

Version: 1.0

## Purpose

The homepage introduces The Villa Tent as a premium luxury tent and hospitality infrastructure brand. It should create trust quickly, showcase key product categories and projects, and guide visitors toward quote, contact, and brochure actions.

## Primary Goals

- Communicate premium hospitality positioning.
- Showcase tent collections.
- Highlight completed projects.
- Generate qualified leads.
- Support SEO for luxury tent and resort tent keywords.
- Build trust through statistics, clients, and project proof.

## SEO Targets

Primary keywords:

- Luxury tent manufacturer
- Resort tent manufacturer
- Safari tent supplier
- Glamping tent solutions
- Hospitality tent structures

Recommended meta title:

```text
Luxury Tent Manufacturer | The Villa Tent
```

Recommended meta description:

```text
Explore premium luxury tents, safari tents, and resort tent solutions for hotels, resorts, glamping sites, and hospitality projects worldwide.
```

## Accessibility

- One `h1` only.
- All images require meaningful alt text.
- Buttons and links must be keyboard accessible.
- Form fields must have labels.
- Focus states must be visible.

## Performance

- Use WebP images.
- Lazy-load below-the-fold images.
- Preload hero image if needed.
- Avoid heavy animation.
- Keep layout stable to reduce CLS.

## Final Rule

The homepage should feel premium, clear, and conversion-focused. It should immediately explain what The Villa Tent offers, prove credibility, and make inquiry actions easy.
