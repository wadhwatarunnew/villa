Project Overview

A Royal Hospitality
Experience

We had the privilege of partnering with The Oberoi Group to create a one-of-a-kind luxury tented resort at Rajgarh Palace, Khajuraho. The project reflects the perfect harmony of regal architecture and world-class glamping hospitality.

Each tent is custom-designed with handcrafted interiors, premium fabrics and modern amenities to ensure unparalleled comfort for guests.

PROJECT GALLERY

CHALLENGE

  • Extreme weather conditions ranging from hot summers to cool winters.
  • Maintaining the royal aesthetic while ensuring modern comfort.
  • Delivering a premium experience with eco-friendly and sustainable materials.

OUR SOLUTION

  • Used high-quality, all-weather fabrics and insulated roofing for maximum comfort.
  • Custom interiors with royal Rajasthani elements blended with luxury furnishings.
  • Sustainable practices and locally sourced materials were incorporated in the project.
